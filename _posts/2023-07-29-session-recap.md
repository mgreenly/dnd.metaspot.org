2023-07-29 Prized Necklace
==========================

7 players for session #53.


It seems this group is definitely a bunch of risk takers.  They certainly knew this tomb was very deadly but they stayed anyway.  They were extremely cautious about what they engaged with and everyone survived.  The big take of the night beyond cash and experience was a necklace of fireballs.

I also had a lot of fun dropping some new NPCs and story elements last night.  All around a great evening.

We're always looking for new players for this group or for a second group I hope to put together soon.  Don't let the scheduling commitment scare you off.  Irregular players are awesome!  Message me if you're interested.
