2023-09-02 The Night Terror
===========================

6 players for session #58.

5 regulars and 1 guest player for Saturday nights D&D game this week.  Continuing the quest to fix the broken sword the party ended up having to negotiate passage through goblin lands.  This resulted in them having to trade a spell and vanquish the Night Terror (displacer beast) plaguing the tribe.  In our game magic is a diminishing resource and can't be created so this meant the magic user actually had to give up a spell.
