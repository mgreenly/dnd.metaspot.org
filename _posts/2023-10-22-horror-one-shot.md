2023-10-22 Horror One Shot
==========================

7 players for the Horror One Shot last night, five of which were new at the table. It was a good time and the players solved the mystery but it’s not my normal style of game and I was a bit under-prepared. We used the Fear Itself rules with a published Call of Cthulhu scenario “And Some Fell on Stoney Ground”, from Nameless Horrors that was augmented a bit to support more players. With a little polish I think that pairing could make a nearly perfect one-shot.
