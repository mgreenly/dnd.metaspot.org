# Barrowkeep

Blue, Damaris, Doco, and  Dwight obtained a charter from Viscount Kell to build a 50' x 50' keep in the [Barrowmounds](/pages/places/barrowmounds.html).  It sits on top of mound #12 which also serves as entrance to the [Barrowmaze](/pages/places/barrowmaze.html).

None of hte original characters are present in the game.  The keep has been taken over by the parties primary characters.  The legal status of the land is definitely a bit fuzzy.
