Looking for Players
===================

If you’re interested in playing D&D or other table top RPG’s and live within driving distance of <a href="https://goo.gl/maps/inpHNH6SvGcQHKe27">Sandstone MN</a>, please read on!

I host a weekly in person game and we're always interested in new players.

We play D&D every Saturday evening and occasionally do one-shots and mini-series events with other systems.

Anyone who wants to play is welcome, no matter if you're a first time player or longtime veteran, please reach out!

The best place to make contact is our Facebook group.
