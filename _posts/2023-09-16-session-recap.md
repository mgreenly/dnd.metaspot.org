# 2023-09-16 Horn of the Dead

6 players for session #60.

One new member to the group tonight and Quinn stopping by to show of her renfest cosplay.  The group survived 4  juju zombies, 1 wight, 4 flagstone golems and blowing the Horn of the Dead, a cursed horn they thought would summon help.  They definitely took some risks but the dice went in their favor and no one died.  The rewards were pretty good also, a +2 mace, a ring of spell turning, a potion of strength and 20 +1 arrows as well as some gold.
