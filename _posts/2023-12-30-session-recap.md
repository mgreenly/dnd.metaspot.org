# 2023-12-30 Soggy Mummies

## Attending

5 players for session #73.

  * Eric  ([Kudo](/pages/pcs/kudo.html)              - half-orc/assasin/2)
  * Jacob ([Sir Lear](/pages/pcs/sirlear.html)       - human/knight/1)
  * Keith ([Lowson](/pages/pcs/lowson.html)          - human/pladin/1)
  * Pete  ([Un'Duhr](/pages/pcs/unduhr.html)         - human/illusionist/3)
  * Quinn ([Valithra](/pages/pcs/valithra.html)      - drow/fighter/3)

## Almanac

  * Today is Hayme, the third day of the 4th week and the 24th day of the 2nd month, in the 615th year of the Great Kingdoms (615/02/24)
  * It's mid-winter, tempatures are chilly, it's cloudy and windy.
  * The moonsign is currently "Waning Dead".  All undead creatures attack anyone born under this moonsign only after attacking all other members of their party.

## Events

**615/02/24 - The Brazen Strumpet**

The group makes its weekly trip into [Helix](/pages/places/helix.html) to replenish supplies.  After they complete their shopping they stop by the Brazen Strumpet for a pint before heading back to the [Barrowkeep](/pages/places/barrowkeep.html).

They overhear the patrons trading stories about the attack on the Scarlet Brotherhood's temple in [Eastdale](/pages/places/eastdale.html).

They hear stories of an exceedingly handsome, dark haired man in gleaming silver and gold armor of antique design single handedly laying waste to dozen or more of [St. Yggs](/pages/gods/stygg.html) greatest champions. 

The party clearly recognizes this description as [Gorastadt](/pages/npcs/gorastadt.html).

They also surmise from the stories that Gorastadt has begun to gather a number of followers and has a growing retinue accompanying him.

The group ponders what would happen if [Ambrose](/pages/npcs/ambrose.html) and Gorastadt met?

Feeling brave after a few pints the party decides to return to mound #34 and continue exploring it in the morning.

They return home to the Barrowkeep before sunset.

**615/02/25 - Barrow Mound #34**

Thane, Pasley, and Kalir join the characters for this outing.

When the party arrives at the mound they discover it just as they had left it. Several small scavengers and carrion birds were feeding on the zombie bodies that were left to rot but scattered as they approached.

Pasley cautiously looks for traps and listens at the door but only hears dripping water.

Impatiently Vilithra reaches over Pasley's head and pushes the door open. The torch light exposes a fairly large room with knee deep muddy water and a raised platform at the back.  The ceiling is partially collapsed on the north side of the room. On the platform is a large white sarcophagus with a skeletal birds head carved in it.  There are statues of naked male warriors wearing only helms carved from jet black stone to either side of the sarcophagus.

The group starts to move into the room and spread out, Paisley crosses the room to the steps that ascend to the sarcophagus' platform.

Kudo veers off from the group and starts to search towards the south and bumps into something submerged in the water. He probes the object with his dagger and a soggy mummy gently floats to the top, rolls over and then settles back to the bottom.

Just to be safe Lowson attempts to sever the mummy's head. As he strikes it lurches upright and attacks.

The mummy proves to be difficult to harm.  Only the party members with magic weapons seem to do any lasting damage, but they eventually slay it.

Unfortunately Valithra ends up wounded and diseased.

During the fight Un'Duhr bumps into a second mummy in the water and quickly decides to take a few steps back.

The party moves up the stairs to inspect the sarcophagus and statues.  Pasley carefully inspects the area for traps. While trying pry the sarcophagus lid open with a dagger two mummies lurch up out of the water, the second mummy that  Un'Duhr had already discovered and a third, previously unknown mummy on the northern side of the room.

Most of the party quickly formulates the plan to attack the second mummy while party members without magic weapons try to keep the third mummy distracted.

Un'Duhr casts Mirror Image helping to distract the second mummy.

Lowson calls on his sword's extraordinary ability of extra damage (x4 for 1d10 rounds).

After defeating the second mummy, and as they move to engage the third, everyone hears the stone on stone grinding of the sarcophagus lid being pushed open from within.

Valithra activates her sword's extraordinary ability of telekinesis to try and hold the lid shut. This seems to work initially and buys the group enough time to slay the third mummy.

Kudo takes out some rope and starts moving towards the sarcophagus intending to bind the hands of the mummy that's trying to escape, but as he approaches, it manages to overcome Vilithra's attempts to contain it and forces the lid open.

Lowson charges forward and cleaves the mummy in half just as it begins to cast a spell.

  * 1 x Death Mask (20% magic resistance)
  * 1 x Platinum Figurine (2,400gp)
  * 5 x Ivory Figurine (1,250gp)
  * 4 x Healing Salves (8hp)
  * 3 x [Mummy](https://dnd.metaspot.org/pages/creatures/mummy.html) (1,200xp)
  * 1 x Gold Ring (660gp)
  * 1 x [Barrow Mummy](https://dnd.metaspot.org/pages/creatures/mummy-barrow.html) (960xp)

The party returns to the keep and Vilithra consumes the ancient preserved liver in the magic canopic jar to cure her disease. It did **NOT** taste like chicken but it seemed to work.

**615/2/18 - Barrow Mound #33**

Fizpop joins the party for some added muscle as they head out to mound #33

The party arrives at mound #33 to find it recently looted.  While searching it they do discover a golden ring missed by the previous tomb robbers.

**615/2/18 - Barrow Mound #32**

On the way to the mound the party encounters a half dozen juju zombies.  They put up a stubborn fight but the party defeats them without losses, although Fizpop became diseased.

  * 6 x [Juju Zombie](https://dnd.metaspot.org/pages/creatures/zombie-juju.html) (600xp)

We ended here and will resume from where we left off next week.
