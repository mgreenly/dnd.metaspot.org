Juju zombies are fearsome undead opponents.  They are distinguished from other zombies by their dark amber-colored skin.

Juju zombies are stronger, faster, and posses greater capacity for action than standard zombies.  They can use missle weapons and climb like thieves of similar hit dice.

**Special:** Juju zombies attack and are turned as 6HD monsters.

**Undead:** Makes no noise until they attack.

**Invulnrable:** Only harmed by +1 or better weapons. Fire does half damage. Electricity, Magic Missle and Cold based attacks do no damage.

**Immune** Things that only affect the living have no effect (e.g. poison) have no effect.

**Immune** Mind-affecting or mind-reading spells (e.g. charm, hold, sleep) have no effect.

**Attribution:** Barrowmaze
