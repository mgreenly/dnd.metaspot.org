2023-07-15 Valiant Stand
========================

4 players for session #51.

The party decided to try something new hoping it would be easy but they ran into a Greater Crypt Shade right off the bat.  Most of the party decided to make a run for it but Ashley and Jacob's characters stood their ground and defeated it.  The night wasn't all successes though.  Eric's acrobat ran out of luck, but that was nearly inevitable.  He only had 1 HP.
