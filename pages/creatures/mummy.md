An undead humanoid wrapped in funerary bandages.

**Paralysis:** Anyone seeing a mummy must save versus paralysis or be paralysed with terror.  Paralysis is broken if the mummy attacks or goes out of sight.

**Disease:** Anyone hit contracts a horrible rotting disease.  Magical healing is ineffective; natural healing is ten times slower.  The disease can only be removed by magic.

**Invulnrable:** Only harmed by magic or fire.  All damage reduced by half.

**Undead:** Makes no noise until they attack.

**Immune** Things that only affect the living have no effect (e.g. poison) have no effect.

**Immune** Mind-affecting or mind-reading spells (e.g. charm, hold, sleep) have no effect.

**Attribution:** Old School Essentials
