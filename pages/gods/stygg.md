# St. Ygg

  * **symbol:** A red cross on a white field.
  * **alignment** Law
  * **portfolio:** Duty, Loyalty, Obedience
  * **domain:** Law, Protection, Strength
  * **weapon:** Greatsword

St. Ygg believes in the highest moral standards.  He is self-righteous and possesses an indignant temper with those who fail to see the just nature of his actions.
