# Silvanus

  * **symbol:** Face of Leaves
  * **aligment:** Neutral
  * **portfolio:** Wild Nature
  * **domain:** Animal, Plant, Protection, Renewal, Water 
  * **weapon:** The Great Mallet of Silvanus

Also called the Green Man, he oversees the balance of nature. Silvanaus has been worshiped since the arrival of man.  The worship of Silvanus has mostly been replac by [St. Ygg](/pages/gods/stygg.html).  Druids and Rangers still look to Salvanus for guidance and protection.
