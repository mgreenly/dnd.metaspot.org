2023-09-30 Alchemist's Tomb
===========================

7 players for session #62.

The group returned to the Barrowmaze for the first time in quite a while.  Most of the current characters have never been there and Dwight was off getting his blindness cured so Ashley Bozman ended up taking the lead with Niffler. In the photo she's explaining the map for Josh Ziegler who's never been into the maze yet.  They encountered some rival adventurers, zombies and skeletons and found an alchemist's tomb with some magical scrolls and potions.  Nice haul for the night.
