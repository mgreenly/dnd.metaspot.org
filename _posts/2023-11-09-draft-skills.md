Deflect
=======
In addition to any attack, the weilder of this weapon may attempt to deflect the number of melee 
and thrown weapon attacks indicated in one round.  To defeclt each attack the character must make 
a savin gthrow vs death rays.

Disarm
======
The weilder of this weapon may attempt to sdisarm an aopponenet instead of making a normal attack.  The 
attacker must roll to hit the target.  The victim can save his weapon by rolling less than or equal to
 his dexterity on 1d20.  If the attacker has the disarm combat option the victim must add a +5 panalty 
to his dies roll.  The DM should determine Dexterity scores for NPCs and monsters or else assume a 
dexterity score of 11.  In addition for each level of master the attacker has gained beyond basic, 
the vicims suffers a penalty of _1 to his rll vs the effect.
