# Helix

Helix, or more specifically, the tavern in Helix, was the starting point for this entire campaign.

The village of Helix is located west of [Ironguard Motte](pages/places/ironguard-motte.html), south of the Blackened Forest on the northern edge of the Barrowmoor.

The worship of [St. Ygg](/pages/gods/stygg.html) is common in Helix and the surrounding area and officially sponsered by local authorities..

The worship of [Herne](/pages/gods/herne.html) is discouraged by the local authorities but still occurs here.
