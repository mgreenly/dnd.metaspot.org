# 2023-11-18 Gorastadt's Tomb

7 players for session #68.

  * Alex (Dwight)
  * Ashley (Nifler & Arma)
  * Eric (Nim)
  * Jacob (Jason)
  * Keith (Grem)
  * Pete (Un'Duhr)
  * Quinn (Dolly)

### 01/12/615

The party gathers at the Brazzen Strumpet.

Dolly and Niffler have a conversation with the agent from the Shadowhands.

Jason considers hiring a bunch of adventurerers but the  the group talks him out of it.

Jason hires Maxwell 1gp/day + 1/2 share.

### 01/13/615

They enter the mounds through the new entrance. (mound #16)

The party encounters Ravenous Zombies.<sup><a href="post/2023/11/18/behind-the-screen.html#zombie-ravenous">[1]</a></sup> (29:00)

Nim turns the zombies and they flee.  Arma taunts them as they run away.

The party goes to get blessed at shrine of Mithra.

The party escorts Dwight to the multi-colored pool room<sup><a href="post/2023/11/18/behind-the-screen.html#lazaruspit">🔗</a></sup>
in barrowmaze so that he can recuperate from his lost constitution.

The party departs for Gorostadt's Tomb in the moors to the south. (41:00)

### 01/14/615
