2023-10-07 Zombie Horde
=======================

6 players for session #63.

The group moved into their new home, the keep on the barrow-moors, and hired staff for it.  Then returned to the barrow-maze for more exploration and to get another haul of books from the alchemist's tomb.  It was a busy night with lots of encounters, the highlights were a huge horde of zombies they trapped between some rooms and their first run-in with exploding skeletons.  Zug was critically injured in combat and will be out for a bit but they stumbled across a replacement, Larry Croft the tomb robber.
