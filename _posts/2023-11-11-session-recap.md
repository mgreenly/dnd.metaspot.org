2023-11-11 Mirror, Mirror
=========================

6 players for session #67.

  * Ashley (Nifler & Arma)
  * Eric (Nim)
  * Jacob (Jason)
  * Keith (Grem)
  * Pete (Un'Duhr)
  * Quinn (Vilithra)

**01/05/615** - A frigid, clear, windy, winter day.  The group goes to town for supplies and stops by the Brazen Strumpet for a pint before heading home.  The tavern is packed, Viscount Kell has stopped in town and the locals have gathered to meet him.  After a few pints the group decides that heading back into the Barrowmaze to collect more books from the Alchemists Tomb is the right choice.

**01/06/615** - In the morning the group prepares their gear and gathers at the entrance to the maze.  They descend into the maze (room #1) and make there way to Mitra's shrine (77A) via the normal route (1, 4, 18, 59, 62, 77a) without incident.  They each take their turn paying homage to Mitra and being blessed.

The group decides to explore the nearest unknown hall, to room 80.  They discover the hall is blocked by a portcullis of heavy iron bands.  Beyond it is a rotted yellow curtain.  Duhr reaches through with his spear to tear down the curtain.  As it falls he sees a mirror beyond, when his gaze falls on it the party witnesses him vanish, and appear in the mirror staring back at the party.  Then a duplicate of Duhr appears from behind him and attacks the original before he can get his bearing.  The original Duhr is slain and at that moment the mirror goes opaque. The group covers the portcullis with the fallen curtain so the mirror can't be seen.  Realizing there's nothing they can do for Duhr the party moves on.

When they move to explore 73D5 they hear a voice from around the corner (room 79).  The party meets Unduhr, a magic-user abandoned in the maze by a group of adventurers from Bogtown.  Unduhr can't get to the party because of the open pit filled with zombies.  They direct him to go around, west to the hall by 78Q3 and back.  While doing this he encounters a lone Stirge (13xp) that dives at him.  Unduhr panics and casts his only spell, Sleep and the stirge falls to the ground where he dispatches it with his staff.

The group then explores 78D5 where they find a statue of a gargoyle and a sarcophagus.  They pry open the sarcophagus and are attacked by the Mummy (400xp) that was inside.  Vilithra uses her swords telekinesis to drop the sarcophagus on the mummy. Once trapped they douse the mummy with holly water and stab at it through the sarcophagus killing it.  They take a golden headband (600gp) and sceptre with large opals (1,600gp) from the mummy.

They move to 81A where they find 7 Fossilized Skeletons (7x20xp) and burial alcoves.  When the fight begins the door to 81A slams shut.  When they search the alcoves they find coins (58gp) and diamond ring (500gp).

They force the door to 81A open and find 3 large dog size Blood Spiders (3x25xp). They have long spindly black legs and bulbous black abdomens with what appears to be a white skull marking it.  They kill the spiders.  There's no loot. Nifler searches the spiders for poison sacks but is unable to locate any.

They explore the stair.  It leads to mound 16.  They had previously discovered these stairs from the top but never explored them.

While exploring room 82 they see a red glow under the door.  Jason drinks his giant strength potion, tears down the door, picks it up and throws it down the hall at 3 of 5 Fire Beetles (5x15xp).  He rolls a 18 on a 3d6 damage roll continuing his amazing streak of rolls with his d6 dice.  They kill the fire beetles there is no treasure.

They open the door to 84 and find 3 pools of colored liquids, red, black and purple.  They fill waterskins with each.

They return to the alchemists tomb and take the last of the books.

They exit the maze without incident.

At the barrowkeep, Grem detects that the colored liquids are magic.

**01/07/615** - They travel to Helix and pay Mazzahs to identify the liquids.  They learn that if they mix all three liquids together and take a drink it will raise that characters prime attribute by one.  Each in the party does so.

**01/08/615** - Back at the Barrowkeep, Grem settles in and starts to study his new library.  He discovers it covers 5 topics; Alchemy, Archaean History, Archaean Engineering, Archaean Folklore, and The Greenwater Sea.

Group compat experience; 13 + 400 + 140 + 75 + 75 = 703 xp.

Group treasure; 600 + 1,600 + 58 + 500 = 2,758 gp.
