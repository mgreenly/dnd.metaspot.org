2023-08-26 A Man Named Ambrose
==============================

5 players for session #57.

Some interesting encounters on the quest to save the sword tonight.  They met a man named Ambrose and had some philosophical discussions about the nature of gods as they shared the road.  Then they stumbled on to a blue dragon trying to eat it's dinner.  I thought maybe Ambrose would have to save them but it turns out the magic user's necklace of fireballs was all they needed.
