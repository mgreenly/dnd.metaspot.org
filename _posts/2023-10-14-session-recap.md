2023-10-14 Surveying the Moors
==============================

5 players for session #64.

The group decided to explore the swamp around their new home to get the lay of the land.   It proved to be pretty dangerous.  They encountered another chimera, a huge army of giant killer frogs, a few crocodiles and a tomb guarded by some gargoyles.  The ranger went down but was brought back by the cleric.
